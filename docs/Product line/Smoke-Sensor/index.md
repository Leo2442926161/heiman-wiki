# Smoke Sensor

Heiman Smoke Sensor uses **photoelectric smoke detection technology** to sensitively detect visible smoke particles at the early stage of a fire, enabling ultra-early fire warning.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Certification | Target Market |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| HS1SA Series | Zigbee / Z-Wave | EN 14604 | EU |
| HS3SA Series | Zigbee | EN 14604 | EU |
| HS8SA | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | UL 217 | US |
| S1 Series | Sub-1G Standalone | EN 14604 | Universal |
| HS1SA-M | Matter over Thread | EN 14604 | Global |

## Core Features

- Photoelectric smoke detection, detects smoke from smoldering and open flames
- 85 dB high-volume audible and visual alarm
- Low battery reminder
- Self-test (one-button test)
- Tamper alarm
- Supports remote silence

## Technical Specifications (HS1SA-M as example)

- Sensor type: Infrared photoelectric
- Alarm sound pressure: ≥85 dB (at 3m)
- Power supply: CR123A battery
- Battery life: ~3 years
- Operating temperature: -10°C ~ +50°C
- Operating humidity: ≤95% RH
