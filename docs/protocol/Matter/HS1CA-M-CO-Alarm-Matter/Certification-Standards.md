# Certification Standards

HS1CA-M has obtained the following certifications:

- **EN 50291** — European standard for carbon monoxide alarms, see [EN European Standards](../../../../standard/EN-European-Standards.md)
- **CE Marking** — EU Declaration of Conformity, see [CE EU Conformity](../../../../standard/CE-EU-Conformity.md)
- **Matter Certification** — Certified by the CSA Alliance Matter certification, ensuring cross-platform smart home interoperability
