# EN European Standards

EN (European Norm) is a technical standard developed by the European Committee for Standardization (CEN) and the European Committee for Electrotechnical Standardization (CENELEC). The main EN standards relevant to Heiman products are as follows:

## EN 14604 — Smoke Alarm Standard

| Item | Description |
|------|------|
| Scope of Application | Standalone smoke alarm |
| Test Requirements | Photoelectric smoke detection, audible and visual alarm, low battery indication |
| Applicable Markets | EU member states, UK, Switzerland, Norway, etc. |
| Heiman Products | HS1SA-E, HS3SA, HS1SA-M, S1 series, etc. |
| Certification Significance | Meets EU building fire safety regulations, a prerequisite for European market access |

**Test items**: Response time, sound pressure level (≥85 dB/3m), silence function, battery life, temperature cycling, humidity endurance, RF interference immunity, etc.

## EN 50291 — Carbon Monoxide Alarm Standard

| Item | Description |
|------|------|
| Scope of Application | Household carbon monoxide (CO) alarm |
| Test Requirements | Electrochemical CO sensor, concentration-graded alarm |
| Applicable Markets | EU member states, UK |
| Heiman Products | HS1CA-E, C1 series, HS1CA-M, etc. |
| Certification Significance | Ensures reliability of CO leak detection, mandatory installation in some countries |

## Other Related EN Standards

- **EN 62368-1**: Audio/video and IT equipment safety
- **EN 301 489**: Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC)
- **EN 300 220**: Short Range Device (SRD) radio spectrum
- **EN 62479**: Assessment of human exposure to low-frequency electromagnetic fields
