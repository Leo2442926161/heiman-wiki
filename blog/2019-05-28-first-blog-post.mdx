In 2022, Heiman Technology was officially recognized as a national "Little Giant" enterprise (Specialized and New) for its deep technical expertise and innovation capabilities in the smart sensing field.

{/* truncate */}

This national-level recognition is a high affirmation of Heiman's specialization, refinement, differentiation, and innovation. Heiman will continue to deepen its focus on smart fire safety, smart gas safety, smart elderly care, and smart sensing, consistently delivering quality products and services to customers.

Previously, Heiman Technology had already received several authoritative qualifications including National High-Tech Enterprise and Guangdong Province Specialized and New SME certifications.
