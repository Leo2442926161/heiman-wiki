# Water Leak Sensor

The Water Leak Sensor detects water leaks or accumulation, effectively preventing property damage from burst pipes or flooding.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|------|----------|------|
| HS1WL | Zigbee | Basic model, probe detection |
| HS2WL Series | Zigbee | Probe + cable detection, dual channel |
| HS3WL Series | Zigbee | Enhanced version |
| L1 Series | Matter / Zigbee | Matter over Thread version, WWHA certified |

## Core Features

- Real-time water leak/accumulation detection
- Buzzer alarm
- Low battery reminder
- Corrosion-resistant probe design

## Installation Locations

- Under bathroom sink
- Under kitchen sink
- Next to washing machine/dishwasher
- Under water heater
- Basement/garage
- Near radiators

## Scene

- **Leak detection + solenoid valve**: automatically close main valve when leak detected
- **Leak alarm + push notification**: notify property manager or family via phone when away
- **Water leak + smart shutoff**:linkage gas water heater or smart water valve
