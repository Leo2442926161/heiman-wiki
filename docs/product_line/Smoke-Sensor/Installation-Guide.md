# Installation Guide

## Mounting Location Requirements

- **Mount on ceiling**, at the center of the room
- At least **30 cm** away from walls or light fixtures
- **Avoid dead air spaces** (e.g. corners, behind beams)
- **Do not install near**: bathrooms (steam), kitchens (grease), ventilation openings

## Installation Steps

1. Select mounting location, ensure ceiling is flat
2. Rotate and fix the mounting base to the ceiling (using screws or 3M adhesive)
3. Insert battery (note polarity), sensor powers on automatically
4. Rotate and snap the sensor body into the base
5. Press and hold the test button — alarm LED flashes and alarm sounds to confirm normal operation
6. Pair via gateway: enter pairing mode, add device in the APP

## Important Notes

- Test functionality once a month
- Battery life ~3 years, replace promptly when low
- Replace entire sensor every 10 years
- Mounting height does not affect smoke detection — smoke rises to the ceiling
