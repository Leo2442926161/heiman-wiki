# GB Chinese National Standards

GB (Guóbiāo, national standard) is the abbreviation for national standards of the People's Republic of China, formulated by the standardization administrative department of the State Council.

## Main GB Standards for Heiman Products

| Standard No. | Standard Name | Applicable Products |
|----------|----------|----------|
| GB 20517 | Independent Smoke Fire Detector Alarm | Smoke alarm |
| GB 15322.1 | Combustible Gas Detector Part 1: Industrial and Commercial Use | Industrial gas detector |
| GB 15322.2 | Combustible Gas Detector Part 2: Household Use | Household gas detector |
| GB/T 31141 | Household Carbon Monoxide Alarm | CO alarm |
| GB 4706.1 | Safety of Household and Similar Electrical Appliances | Smart plug |

## Relationship between CCC and GB

CCC certification (China Compulsory Certification) is technically based on GB standards. A product that passes CCC certification is verified to comply with the relevant GB standard requirements.
