# Emergency Button

The Emergency Button (Panic Button) is used to quickly trigger aSOS signal in emergency situations, serving as an important component of elderly care and security systems.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|------|----------|------|
| Emergency Button | Zigbee | Portable, one-clickSOS |

## Core Features

- One-touch emergency alarm
- Portable design (can be carried or wall-mounted)
- Low battery reminder
- Anti-accidental-touch design (long press to trigger)

## Applicable Scenarios

- **Elderly care**: one-clickSOS whenelderly suddenly falls ill or falls down
- **Children's room**: for emergencies when child is home alone
- **Medical care**: bedside call in hospital rooms/nursing homes
- **Storefront/reception**: covert alarm trigger during robbery or emergency

## Trigger

- Push notification to family/guardian
- Siren alarm
- Auto-call property management or 120
