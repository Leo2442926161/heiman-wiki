# Gas Detector

The Gas Detector is used to detect leaks of flammable gases such as natural gas (methane) and liquefied petroleum gas (propane), and is a core device for kitchen safety.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Detection Gas | Certification |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| JT-G1 | Standalone | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |
| JT-HS1CG Series | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |
| JT-HS3CG | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |
| JT-HS8CG | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |

## Core Features

- High-stability semiconductor gas sensor
- Leak concentration tiered alarm
- Auto shutoff linkage (with solenoid valve)
- Audible and visual alarm (≥85 dB)
- Self-test function
- Sensor end-of-life reminder

## Scene

- **Leak detected → shutoff valve**: automatically close gas pipeline valve
- **Alarm + exhaust**: automatically turn on exhaust fan / range hood
- **Push notification**: receive leak alarm on phone in real time

## Safety Information

If you smell gas, do NOT operate electrical switches or use open flames. Immediately open windows for ventilation and call emergency services from a safe outdoor area.
