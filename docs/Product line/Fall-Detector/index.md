# Fall Detector

The Fall Detector is designed specifically for the elderly, automatically detecting fall incidents and sendingSOS signals in time, serving as a key device in smart elderly care solutions.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|------|----------|------|
| HS2FD | Zigbee | Smart fall detection, automatic alarm |

## Core Technology

Uses **multi-sensor fusion algorithm**, combining accelerometer and gyroscope data with AI algorithms to determine fall posture, effectively distinguishing falls from normal activities (such as sitting down, bending), reducing false alarm rate.

## Core Features

- Automatic fall detection
- One-touch manualSOS
- Wearable design (belt clip or lanyard)
- Low battery reminder

## Applicable Scenarios

- Home elderly care: 24/7 protection for seniors living alone
- Nursing homes: centralized monitoring and management
- Rehabilitation centers: fall risk monitoring for post-surgery patients
