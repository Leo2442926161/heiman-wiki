Heiman Technology's next-generation Matter smart smoke alarm HS1SA-M has been officially launched, making it one of the first smoke alarm products in the industry to receive Matter 1.2 certification.

{/* truncate */}

:::tip

HS1SA-M supports four major smart home ecosystems: Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Samsung SmartThings.

:::

## Key Features

- **Matter 1.2 Certified** — Cross-brand, cross-ecosystem interoperability
- **Photoelectric Smoke Sensing** — Rapid response to various fire smoke types
- **Voice Alarm Alerts** — Multiple language options for clear hazard communication
- **Self-Test Function** — Periodic self-checks to ensure normal operation
- **Remote Notification** — Real-time push alerts via mobile app

## Compatibility

HS1SA-M seamlessly integrates with mainstream smart home platforms, allowing users to control and manage it directly via their smartphones without the need for an additional gateway.
