# wM-Bus (Wireless Meter-Bus)

wM-Bus (Wireless Meter-Bus) is a European standard (EN 13757) wireless meter reading protocol widely used for remote data collection of energy metering devices such as water meters, gas meters, electricity meters, and heat meters.

## Technical Specifications

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Standard | EN 13757 |
| Operating Frequency | 868 MHz (EU standard) |
| Transmission Range | 100-500 m (open area) |
| Power Consumption | Very low, battery life up to 10 years |
| Primary Application | Energy metering, centralized meter reading |

## Heiman and wM-Bus

Heiman leverages wM-Bus technology to provide solutions for smart gas and smart water utility sectors. wM-Bus products are mainly used in large and medium-sized commercial and industrial projects, in conjunction with the Heiman smart fire protection platform.

## Applicable Scenarios

- Centralized apartment/residential gas meter reading
- Commercial building energy consumption monitoring
- Smart city infrastructure

> **Note**: wM-Bus is a vertical industry protocol primarily used in Heiman's gas and fire protection project solutions, and is not applicable to ordinary home smart home scenarios.
