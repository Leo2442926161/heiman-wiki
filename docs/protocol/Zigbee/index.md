# Zigbee
Zigbee is a wireless communication technology designed specifically for smart home and Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Think of it like Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, but instead of sending high-speed data to your phone or computer, it sends small, low-power signals between smart devices like light bulbs, sensors, and switches.

It is built on a mesh network architecture, which means devices don't just talk to a central router—they talk to each other. Every plugged-in device (like a smart plug or light bulb) acts as a repeater, passing the signal along to the next device. This extends the range and makes the network incredibly stable.


# Why Choose Zigbee?
* If you are building a smart home or developing IoT devices, Zigbee offers several massive advantages over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth:

* Low Power Consumption: Zigbee devices use very little energy. Battery-powered devices like motion detectors, door sensors, and temperature sensors can run for years on a single coin-cell battery.

* Excellent Reliability (Mesh Network): If one smart bulb in your kitchen goes offline, the signal automatically reroutes through another device (like a living room plug) to reach its destination. The more devices you add, the stronger and more resilient your network becomes.

* Doesn't Slow Down Your Wi-Fi: If you connect 50 smart switches and sensors directly to your home Wi-Fi router, your internet connection will likely slow down or drop. Zigbee operates on its own dedicated network via a central hub or gateway, keeping your main Wi-Fi completely clear.

* Local Control: Many Zigbee setups process commands locally through a hub rather than relying on the cloud. This means even if your internet goes down, your smart automation schedules and switches will still work perfectly.

* Highly Secure: Zigbee uses AES-128 encryption, ensuring that the data passing between your smart devices remains safe and secure from outside interference.


# Heiman and Zigbee

Heiman is among the first Chinese manufacturers to adopt the Zigbee protocol, with an extensive Zigbee product line. Heiman Zigbee devices are compatible with the **Zigbee 3.0** standard and can interoperate with mainstream Zigbee gateways and coordinators.
