# CCC China Compulsory Certification

CCC (China Compulsory Certification) is a mandatory product certification system implemented by the Chinese government to protect personal and property safety.

## Scope of Application

CCC certification covers the following Heiman product categories:

| Product Category | CCC Standard | Description |
|----------|----------|------|
| Independent Smoke Fire Detector Alarm | GB 20517 | Smoke alarm CCC mandatory |
| Combustible Gas Detector | GB 15322.2 | Gas leak detection CCC mandatory |
| Carbon Monoxide Detector | Per GB standard | Mandatory for some categories |

## Main GB Standards

### GB 20517 — Independent Smoke Fire Detector Alarm

Heiman smoke alarms with CCC certification comply with GB 20517 requirements and can be directly sold in the Chinese market.

### GB 15322.2 — Combustible Gas Detector Part 2

Heiman JT series and HS1CG series combustible gas detectors are certified to GB 15322.2, meeting Chinese gas alarm market access requirements.

## CCC Mark

Products with CCC certification bear the CCC mark. Users can verify the certification number on the website of the China National Certification and Accreditation Administration.

## Key Differences from EN/UL

| Aspect | CCC | EN | UL |
|------|-----|----|-----|
| Applicable Region | China | EU | USA/Canada |
| Certification Body | CQC etc. | Third-party notified body | UL LLC |
| Standard System | GB standards | EN standards | UL standards |
| Factory Inspection | Initial + annual inspection required | Required | Required |
