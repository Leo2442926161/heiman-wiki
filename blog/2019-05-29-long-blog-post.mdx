Since its founding in 2005, Heiman Technology has journeyed over 20 years of development. This article takes you through Heiman's key milestones.

{/* truncate */}

## 2005 - Company Founded

Shenzhen Heiman Technology Co., Ltd. was officially established, entering the security and sensing field.

## 2006 - International Expansion

Multiple products received EU CE certification, and a partnership was established with China Mobile.

## 2011 - International Certification Breakthrough

Multiple products passed EU EN54-7 and EN14604 certification, marking product quality reaching international advanced standards.

## 2012 - Smart Home Transformation

The smart home project was fully launched, transitioning Heiman into a company focused on R&D, production, and sales of smart home products.

## 2017 - Major Breakthroughs

- Developed China's first NB-IoT smoke alarm
- Won 2 German Red Dot Design Awards
- Obtained MFi certification and Apple manufacturer qualification

## 2020 - Capital Boost

Completed Series A funding round with Shenzhen HTI Group as investor, received "Guangdong Province Specialized and New SME" designation.

## 2022 - National Recognition

Recognized as a national "Little Giant" enterprise; K1 series won German Red Dot Design Award.

## 2023 - International Standards Leadership

Heiman testing laboratory officially passed national CNAS accreditation; smoke alarms and CO alarms were among the first to receive Matter 1.2 certification.

## 2024 - Present

Continuous innovation, deeply engaged in smart fire safety, smart gas safety, smart elderly care, and smart sensing, providing quality products and services to customers in 100+ countries worldwide.
