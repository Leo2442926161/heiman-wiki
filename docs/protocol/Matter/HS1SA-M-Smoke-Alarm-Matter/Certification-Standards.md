# Certification Standards

HS1SA-M has obtained the following certifications:

- **EN 14604** — European standard for smoke alarms
- **CE Marking** — EU Declaration of Conformity
- **Matter Certification** — Certified by the CSA Alliance Matter certification, ensuring cross-platform smart home interoperability
- **Red Dot Design Award** — Red Dot Product Design Award
